Dashboards

Overview

The macro and markets dashboards combine and display economic and financial data collected from the web. The dashboards are generally updated weekly on Saturday.

Featured content

Chartbook

Over 100 indicators of U.S. macroeconomic performance in one pdf file, organized by sector and topic.

Key Series

A selection of key global macroeconomic indicators, presented as interactive web graphics using Bokeh.

ExecDash

One-page summary and high-level overview of recent U.S. macroeconomic and market developments.

Japan Dash

Two-page summary of recent Japan macroeconomic developments, covering labor, industry, prices, and rates.

Unemp MSA

Map of recent changes to unemployment rates by metropolitan statistical area (MSA) with data table.

Trade Growth

(Draft) Interactive look at the most recent U.S. international trade data by partner and product.
