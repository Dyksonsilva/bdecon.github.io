About

BD Economics is a project by Brian Dew to provide useful and timely information on both recent macroeconomic trends and techniques for working with macroeconomic data.

Brian Dew ( @bd_econ; my CV)

I'm an economist/data scientist focused on U.S. economic policy, international trade, network analysis, and open economy macroeconomics. I'm currently a research assistant at the Center for Economic and Policy Research and have worked previously with the International Monetary Fund (IMF) and the Institute of International Finance. I hold a master's degree in economics from American University in Washington, DC and a bachelor's degree in Pyschology and Organizational Sciences from the George Washington University.
